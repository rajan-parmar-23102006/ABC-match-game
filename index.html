How to Play

ABC Match Master is a memory matching game where you need to match uppercase letters with their lowercase equivalents.

  • Click on cards to flip them and reveal letters
  • Match uppercase letters (A-Z) with their lowercase pairs (a-z)
  • Try to complete the game with the fewest moves and in the shortest time
  • Use hints wisely - you only have 3 per game
  • Choose difficulty level: Easy (10 pairs), Medium (20 pairs), or Hard (26 pairs)

Your best scores will be saved to the scoreboard!
